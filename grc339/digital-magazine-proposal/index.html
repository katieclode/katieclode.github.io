Introduction

ISO 800

ISO 800 is a low-light photography magazine which would feature photographs taken in low-light conditions. The magazine would include articles about the photographers and topics such as tutorials or new trends. The name refers to the camera setting ISO, or the sensitivity of the camera's sensor, and 800 is a typical starting point of low-light shooting.
