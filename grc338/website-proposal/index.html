Introduction

Luminate

Luminate is a small jewelry boutique located in downtown San Luis Obispo. The shop sells handmade necklaces, earrings, and rings. Luminate was founded by two Cal Poly alumni who love making jewelry.

Target audience

This site's target audience will be people who wear and buy jewelry in San Luis Obispo. Their primary goals while using the site will be to browse products and find more information about the store. They would visit the site because they are looking to buy jewelry from the store but they want to look at products and learn more about the business first.
